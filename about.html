About Pathos

Pathos is an LLM powered news sentiment engine that curates articles based on your emotional preferences. Whether you're looking for uplifting stories to brighten your day or thought-provoking content to expand your mind, Pathos delivers personalized news that matches your mood.

Purpose

In an era where news often casts a shadow over our daily lives with relentless negativity, we believe it's time to reclaim our emotional well-being. Pathos' mission is to transform news consumption from a source of stress into a personalized journey that aligns with your feelings. This project allows you to curate your news, ensuring that staying informed enhances your day and nurtures a positive outlook.

The Experience

Each mood selection is carefully crafted to evoke specific feelings and memories. From the tranquility of a beach day to the wonder of discovering Narnia, we've designed each option to transport you to a particular emotional state.

How It Works

Behind the scenes, Pathos uses sophisticated algorithms to analyze news content and match it with your selected mood. We combine artificial intelligence with human curation to ensure the most relevant and emotionally resonant articles reach you.
